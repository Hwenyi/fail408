## 题15
### 题目
> [!error]+
> Q:(15) 设连续函数 $f\left( x\right)$ 满足 $f\left( {x + 2}\right) - f\left( x\right) = x,{\int }_{0}^{2}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = 0$ ,则 ${\int }_{1}^{3}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x =$
### 分析
> [!NOTE]+
> A:这个题目很关键，没想明白我的做法错在哪里，我用的定积分积分变量和积分无关的性质，但是正确的做法应该是拆分1~3上的区间
> ![](https://img.hwenyi.live/202412091817519.webp)
### 解
> [!done]-
> 解 注意到 ${\int }_{2}^{3}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( {x + 2}\right) \mathrm{d}x$ ,故由 $f\left( {x + 2}\right) - f\left( x\right) = x$ 可得,
> $$
> {\int }_{2}^{3}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x - {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( {x + 2}\right) \mathrm{d}x - {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{0}^{1}x\mathrm{\;d}x = \frac{1}{2}. \tag{1}
> $$
> 又因为 ${\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x + {\int }_{1}^{2}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{0}^{2}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = 0$ ,所以 $- {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{1}^{2}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x$ . 从而由 (1) 式可得
> $$
> {\int }_{2}^{3}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x + {\int }_{1}^{2}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2},
> $$
> 即 ${\int }_{1}^{3}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}$ .
