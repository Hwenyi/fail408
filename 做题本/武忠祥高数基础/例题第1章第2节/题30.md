## 题30
[[2024高等数学基础篇.pdf#page=34&selection=275,0,277,1|高等数学基础篇, page 34，题30]]

![](https://img.hwenyi.live/202401182139193.webp)
洛必达，上下同时求导即可
$$
\frac{-\tan(x-1)}{-\frac{\pi}{2}\cdot\cos\frac{\pi}{2}\cdot x}=\frac{-(x-1)}{\frac{\pi}{2}\cos\frac{\pi}{2}x}=\frac{2}{\pi}\frac{x-1}{\cos\frac{\pi}{2}x}=\frac{2}{\pi}\cdot\frac{1}{-\frac{\pi}{2}\sin\frac{\pi}{2}x}=-\frac{4}{{\pi}^{2}}
$$
