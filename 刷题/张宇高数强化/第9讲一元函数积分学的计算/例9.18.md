### 题目

### 解
【解】 $\int {\sin }^{2}x{\cos }^{3}x\mathrm{\;d}x\frac{t = \sin x}{}\int {t}^{2}( {1 - {t}^{2}}) \mathrm{d}t = \int ( {{t}^{2} - {t}^{4}}) \mathrm{d}t = \frac{{t}^{3}}{3} - \frac{{t}^{5}}{5} + C = \frac{{\sin }^{3}x}{3} - \frac{{\sin }^{5}x}{5} + C$ .
