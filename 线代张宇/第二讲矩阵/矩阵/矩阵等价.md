title: 矩阵等价
created: "2024-10-17 04:34"
updated: "2024-10-17 04:34"
---
设 $\mathbf{A},\mathbf{B}$ 均是 $m {\times} n$ 矩阵,若存在[[逆矩阵|可逆矩阵]] ${\mathbf{P}}_{mem},{\mathbf{Q}}_{nen}$ ,使得 $\mathbf{P}\mathbf{A}\mathbf{Q} = \mathbf{B}$ ,则称 $\mathbf{A},\mathbf{B}$ 是等价矩阵,记作 $A {\cong} B.$
