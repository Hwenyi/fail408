title: 梯度的运算法则
---
$\operatorname{grad}(u {\pm} v) = \operatorname{grad}u {\pm} \operatorname{grad}v$ 

$\operatorname{grad}(uv) = v\operatorname{grad}u + u\operatorname{grad}v;$

$\operatorname{grad}\left( \frac{u}{v} \right) = \frac{v\operatorname{grad}u {-} u\operatorname{grad}v}{v^{2}}(v {\neq} 0).$
