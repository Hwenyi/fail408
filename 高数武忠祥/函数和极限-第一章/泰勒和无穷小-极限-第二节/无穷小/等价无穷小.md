Q:两组等价无穷小的和差在**什么条件下**，仍是等价无穷小
aa若 $\alpha\sim\alpha_1,\beta\sim\beta_1,$ 且 $\lim\frac{\alpha_1}{\beta_1}=A\neq1,$ 则 $\alpha-\beta\sim\alpha_1-\beta_1.$
若 $\alpha\sim\alpha_1,\beta\sim\beta_1,$ 且 $\lim\frac{\alpha_1}{\beta_1}=A\neq-1,$ 则 $\alpha+\beta\sim\alpha_1+\beta_1.$
