Q:曲面绕y轴旋转一周的体积
A:![](https://img.hwenyi.live/202403192150852.webp)
$dV=2\pi rd\sigma$
$=2\pi xd\sigma$
对dV取二重积分得到V 
$V_{y}=\iint_{D}2\pi xd\sigma$
$=2\pi\int_a^bdx\int_{y_1(x)}^{y_2(x)}xdy$
$=2\pi\int_a^bx[y_2(x)-y_1(x)]dx$
